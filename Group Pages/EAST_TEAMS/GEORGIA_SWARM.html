Meet the Swarm

The Georgia Swarm are a professional lacrosse founded in 2004, currently based in Duluth, Georgia. They play in the National Lacrosse League (NLL) and are former league champions, having won the Champion's Cup in 2017. The team plays its home games at the Gas South Arena and has a dedicated fan base in the Atlanta metropolitan area.
